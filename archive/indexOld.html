About

I am a UX/UI designer whose background includes pharmacy, classroom teaching and multimeda design. My journey to UX started with a desire to address large scale public health issues in South Korea through accessible technology.

I've worked and interned at organizations like Allscripts, Mad*Pow, and University of Michigan. I studied Human-Computer Interaction and behavioral science through a dual degree at University of Michigan's School of Information and School of Public Health.

When not designing user experiences at work, I like to hang out with my dog, take local classes (currently ceramics and swing dancing), and watch baking shows. I'm usually working on some side projects to hone my skills in sketching, watercolors, digital art, and jquery.
